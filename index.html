Ranking Areas by Water Scarcity

Project Overview

This project leverages the merge sort algorithm to rank urban areas based on water scarcity levels, aiming to optimize resource allocation and ensure equitable distribution.

Methodology

We implemented the merge sort algorithm to efficiently sort areas by their water scarcity index, ensuring accurate and timely rankings.

Results

The sorted list of areas enables targeted interventions in the most affected regions, promoting efficient water management.
